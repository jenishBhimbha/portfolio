Welcome to Cosimo

Welcome to Cosimo. We are a product-focused consultancy dedicated to providing best-in-class Salesforce advisory, implementation and product management services.

We recognised a gap in the market for a dynamic consultancy that offers continuous Salesforce strategic planning and product development. To bridge this gap, we’ve crafted a unique model that provides a permanent and flexible team of Salesforce consultants and product management experts, focused on delivering incremental value through agile practices.

While continuous product development is at our core, we also provide comprehensive Salesforce implementations and change management solutions ensuring you are set up for success.

Join us on a journey to transform your Salesforce products with a consultancy that prioritises outcomes, excellence, and user satisfaction.
